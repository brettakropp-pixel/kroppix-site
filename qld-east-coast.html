Queensland East Coast

A loose road journey from Bargara north through cane fields, ranges, island water and bright tropical coast.

This set is less about one place than the movement between them — the way the colour, weather and distance shift as the road heads north.

Bargara Mackay Eungella Proserpine Townsville Magnetic Island

First light on the coast

The journey begins low and open, with the coastline already pulling north.

Stop 01

Bargara

Blue water, pale sand and the settled edge of the coast before the road stretches further north.

North through ranges and water

The coast gives way to rainforest, rock pools and quieter inland places.

Stop 02

Finch Hatton and Eungella

A cooler shift in the trip — green valleys, running water and small moments of stillness.

Open distance

The landscape stretches out again, with mountains sitting low across water and haze.

Stop 03

Lake Proserpine

A stiller part of the road — less coastal brightness, more distance and weather.

Into the tropics

The colour lifts again around Townsville — harder light, dry rock and bright blue water.

Stop 04

Townsville

A higher, brighter section of the trip where the coast starts to feel more tropical.

Island pause

Magnetic Island slows the page down again — water, animals, and a longer look toward the horizon.

Stop 05

Magnetic Island

The road briefly leaves the road — across water, into heat, granite, wildlife and jetty lines.
